How to get access

Simple steps for everyone at Dhwani RIS

1

Find your name

Search above or scroll through the cards. If your name is listed, you already have access through your team leader.

2

Need access?

Talk to your team leader. They manage who is on each account and can add you if there is space.

3

Team leaders

To add someone to your account, check capacity first. Then add them and update Ravi or Ankit so this page stays current.

4

Questions?

Reach out to Ravi or Ankit on Teams. They manage the accounts centrally.

Tip: Set up an email forwarding rule

Ask your account owner to set up a forwarding rule on the shared account email (e.g. cursor@dhwaniris.com) so login codes and alerts go to everyone on the account automatically. No more chasing OTPs.
